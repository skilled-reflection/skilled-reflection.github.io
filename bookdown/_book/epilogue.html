Glossary

9.5 c2.SELF

  1. A GOAL names an ideal future reality, comprises a PLAN to increase its likelihood, associated with CHUD.
  2. A PLAN is any combination of WORDS where the writing or reading of them aims to improve the chance or decrease the COST of achieving your GOAL.
  3. What is materially required–muscle energy, cash, brain power–is COST.
  4. COST is any action that has a direct exchange.
  5. FOOD is the necessary and sufficient material to satisfy these needs.
  6. CAKE is anything (else) fulfilling, that you are inclined to pursue, including the reason for living.
  7. SELF is what’s necessary and sufficient for FOOD and to maximize BET outcomes (c5.28).
  8. HOME is the collection of materials and TOOLS needed for SELF.
  9. HOME also includes all physical material and resources you own, including MONEY, and environment and basis for HABITS.

9.6 c3.PRI

  1. The GOAL of PRI is to manage the set of GOALS in your life.
  2. TIME is the constant (or denominator) for FORCES, FORGETTING, PRI, and LIB.

9.7 c4.PPL

  1. Money is traded for maintenance of, or insurance for SELF and CAKE, like apples, miracle medical procedures, and a toilet to take your poop somewhere else.
  2. WORK is performing a specific task on a collective garden, like an institution’s in exchange for money.
  3. NORMS are all actions assumed of (or about) the “average” person. They are the web of FORCES of all actions of all PPL, including WORK, religion, popular attitudes, and DOUBTS.
  4. Language, agreed usage of WORDS, is made from NORMS.
  5. NORMS push against individuality.
  6. A FORCE is any cause of change. A FORCE underlies every action involved in a GOAL, yours or otherwise. There are forces within your control, and forces outside.
  7. MAINTENANCE is the COST of FORCE to neither move toward nor away from a GOAL.
  8. ALIGNMENT is when NORM or natural FORCE causes your GOAL to be more likely. To ALIGN is to adjust your GOAL to be more like another force, usually one acting against your GOAL.
  9. Relationships (RLTP) are GOALS about PPL (PPL).

9.8 c5.BET

  1. A BET is a PLAN template for reconciling REALITY with CHUD, to accomplish GOALS.

  2. CHUD encompasses the changes in you, the world, and your GOAL for you to achieve it.

  3. COST c2.51

  4. HABITS are your dominant tendencies (FORCES). 43.1. HABITS are your most likely and reliable FOOD or CAKE actions.

  5. If you have not arrived at the GOAL, there are UNKNOWNs.

  6. DOUBTS are predictable failures of estimating Reality, PLANs and UNKNOWNs.

9.9 c6.ed

  1. Students are PPL with IDEAS from EXPERIENCE toward selfish GOALS.
  2. A reader is a listener and observer.
  3. BET on WORDS. A writer invests TIME and energy to map feelings onto WORDS.
  4. Revisions also make you a WRITER. When you revise WORDS (yours or others’) to maximize your BET, you are a writer.
  5. An instructor READS, and enforces BETs on LINKs toward a PLAN.
